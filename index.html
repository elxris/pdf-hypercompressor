MRC PDF builder — render + OCR + segment + assemble, all client-side

Renders each page (mupdf-wasm), OCRs it (tesseract), segments + encodes the MRC layers in a Pyodide worker pool, and assembles a searchable, compressed PDF — then downloads it. Nothing leaves your machine.
